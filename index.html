Research

My research focusses broadly on developing algorithms for efficient and scalable machine learning systems. I am particularly interested in efficient and hardware-friendly deep learning and its applications in vision, language, and speech using the edge computing paradigm. I am excited about grounding my research in applications relating to accelerating science and engineering. My research is supported by Badger Technologies. Prior to joining Clarkson University, I worked at Samsung R&D. I was working on Samsung IoT projects.
